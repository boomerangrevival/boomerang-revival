Our story

We kept seeing good businesses ignore their best asset.

Spend any time around local service businesses and you notice the same pattern. Owners pour money into chasing brand-new customers — ads, signage, social — while a goldmine of people who already know and trust them sits untouched in their booking software.

Those past customers didn't leave angry. Life got busy. A reminder never came. They simply forgot. And more often than not, a short, friendly, well-timed message is all it takes to bring them back through the door.

So we built Boomerang Revival around that single, stubborn truth: your past customers are your next paycheck. Instead of charging big retainers up front, we tie our success to yours — we run the campaign, and you pay from the revenue it brings back.

It keeps us honest, it keeps our incentives pointed in the same direction as yours, and it makes the decision to work with us an easy one. We're not a faceless vendor. We're local, we answer the phone, and we treat your list and your brand like they're our own.

What we stand for

The principles behind how we work

Skin in the game

We're paid from results. If the campaign doesn't bring customers back, we don't win either. That alignment is the whole point of the company.

Honest & compliant

No spam, no shady tactics. We follow consent and opt-out best practices on every send, because your reputation is far more valuable than any one campaign.

Respect for your brand

Everything goes out in your voice, under your name. We'd rather send a smaller, cleaner campaign than anything that makes your business look pushy.
