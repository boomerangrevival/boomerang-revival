About us

Helping local businesses collect what they've already earned

Boomerang Revival is a small, founder-led team based in Tallahassee, Florida. We exist for one reason: to help local service businesses turn their forgotten customer lists back into revenue.

Our story

We kept seeing the same thing.

Local businesses pour money into finding brand-new customers — ads, signage, social — while a goldmine of people who already know and trust them sits untouched in their booking software.

Those past customers stopped coming back for ordinary reasons. Life got busy. A reminder never came. They simply forgot. They didn't leave because they were unhappy — they just drifted. And a short, friendly, well-timed message is often all it takes to bring them back.

So we built Boomerang Revival around that idea. Instead of charging big retainers up front, we tie our success to yours: we run the campaign, and you pay from the revenue it brings back. It keeps us honest and makes saying yes easy.

We're not a faceless agency. We're local, we answer the phone, and we treat your list and your brand with the care they deserve.

Our mission

Help local businesses recover money they already earned.

Every returning customer is proof that good work pays twice. We want every local owner we work with to feel the relief of seeing their calendar fill up again — without taking on risk to do it.

Skin in the game

We're paid from results. If the campaign doesn't bring customers back, we don't win either. That's the whole point.

Honest & compliant

No spam, no shady tactics. We follow consent and opt-out best practices so your reputation stays protected.

Local & hands-on

Based in Tallahassee and built for businesses like yours. Real people you can reach, not a ticket queue.
