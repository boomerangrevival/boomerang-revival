Everything you're probably wondering

Straight answers on cost, payment, compliance and what's expected of you. Don't see your question? Just ask us.

There's no big upfront retainer to test database reactivation. Our model is built so you pay primarily from the revenue the campaign actually generates — meaning the offer only really costs you once it has already made you money. We'll walk you through the exact structure on your free audit, with no surprises and nothing hidden.
We tie our compensation to results. When the reactivation campaign brings customers back and generates revenue, we're paid a share of that recovered revenue. Because we only win when you win, our incentive is to make the campaign perform — not to lock you into a long contract. Exact terms are agreed up front before anything goes live.
It's the opposite of spam. We message your own past customers — people who already did business with you — with a genuinely useful offer, in your brand voice. Every send follows messaging best practices: clear identification, easy opt-out, and respect for consent and contact rules. Protecting your reputation is non-negotiable; we'd rather send a smaller, cleaner campaign than risk your brand.
Fast. Most campaigns go live within days of kickoff, and because you're messaging people who already know you, replies and bookings often start coming in within the first 24–72 hours. This isn't a slow-burn brand-building play — it's designed to put appointments on the calendar quickly.
Smaller lists work surprisingly well. Each past customer is worth real revenue when they rebook, so even a few hundred contacts can more than justify a campaign.
